About the Centre

Centre of Water and Air Analysis

The Centre for Water and Air Analysis at Adamas University was established to promote scientific research, environmental monitoring, and sustainable solutions to contemporary environmental challenges. It provides advanced analytical support for assessing water and air quality through modern instrumentation and evidence-based studies.

The Centre functions as an academic and research platform under Adamas University, supporting interdisciplinary research, consultancy, training, and community outreach. It contributes significantly to environmental education and capacity building through student training and collaborative initiatives.

The Centre focuses on pollution monitoring, ecological assessments, biodiversity studies, and environmental impact analysis. It also supports industrial and government projects and is committed to fostering environmental sustainability through scientific excellence and innovative research.

Our Vision

  • To become a leading centre of excellence in water and air quality analysis, fostering sustainable environmental practices and contributing to global efforts in mitigating pollution.
  • To promote advanced knowledge and research in environmental monitoring and pollution science.
  • To encourage innovative technologies and best practices in water and air quality management.
  • To develop sustainable and cost-effective solutions for environmental protection and public health.
  • To strengthen industry–academia collaboration in the field of environmental science.
  • To contribute to global efforts in mitigating pollution through advanced research and community engagement.

Our Mission

  • To provide accurate, reliable, and advanced analytical services for assessing water and air quality, catering to academic, industrial, and societal needs.
  • To advance scientific understanding of environmental challenges by promoting interdisciplinary research and innovation.
  • To educate and empower communities, industries, and stakeholders in adopting sustainable practices for clean water and air.
  • To build capacity among students and researchers by offering training and knowledge-sharing programs in environmental monitoring and analysis.
Prof. Suranjan Das Vice Chancellor, Adamas University

What We Aim to Achieve

Objectives

01

Environmental Monitoring and Assessment

To monitor and evaluate the quality of water and air in urban, peri-urban, and rural areas, identifying pollution sources and trends.

02

Research and Development

To conduct cutting-edge research on air and water pollution, exploring innovative technologies for mitigation and management.

03

Capacity Building

To organize training programs, workshops, and seminars for students, researchers, policymakers, and industry professionals.

04

Community Engagement

To create awareness about water and air quality issues among the public, promoting sustainable behaviour and practices.

05

Policy Support

To provide scientific data and recommendations for formulating evidence-based environmental policies and regulations.

Laboratory Infrastructure

Instruments Used in the Centre

🔬

Atomic Absorption Spectrophotometer

Used for quantitative analysis of trace and heavy metals in environmental samples. Supports water and air quality assessment and toxic metal detection.

Environmental Monitoring
⚗️

UV-Vis Double Beam Spectrophotometer

Used for quantitative analysis of chemical species in environmental samples. Supports water quality assessment, pollutant detection, and aquatic system studies.

Environmental Monitoring
♨️

Autoclave

Sterilization instrument using high-pressure steam to decontaminate laboratory media, glassware, and biological materials, ensuring aseptic conditions.

Laboratory Equipment

BOD Incubator

Maintains controlled-temperature conditions for environmental and microbiological sample incubation. Supports BOD studies and organic pollution analysis.
