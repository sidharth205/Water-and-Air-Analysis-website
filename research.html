Research & Activities

Exploring the frontiers of environmental science through rigorous research, analytical services, and interdisciplinary collaboration.

What We Do

Activities & Services

🌿

Environmental Research

Conducting interdisciplinary research in environmental science, pollution control, climate change, waste management, and sustainability.

🦋

Ecological Studies

Biodiversity assessment, ecosystem monitoring, ecological surveys, conservation studies, and environmental impact assessment.

🎓

Student Training & Internship Support

Offering hands-on training, laboratory exposure, research guidance, dissertation support, and internships for students.

🤝

Collaborative Projects

Promoting collaborations with industries, NGOs, research institutions, and government bodies for sustainable environmental solutions.

📢

Awareness & Outreach Programs

Organizing workshops, seminars, field studies, plantation drives, sustainability campaigns, and environmental awareness activities.

💧

Water Quality Analysis

Assessment of physical, chemical, and biological parameters of drinking water, groundwater, surface water, wastewater, and industrial effluents.

💨

Air Quality Monitoring

Monitoring and analysis of indoor and outdoor air pollutants, particulate matter, gaseous pollutants, and environmental exposure assessment.

🔬

Consultancy and Testing Services

Providing analytical and consultancy support to industries, organizations, researchers, and government agencies for environmental monitoring and compliance.

📊

Data Analysis and Reporting

Preparation of scientific reports, environmental data interpretation, and documentation for research and regulatory purposes.

What We Deliver

Research Deliverables

The Centre delivers outcomes across education, research, consultancy, and industry collaboration to address critical environmental challenges.

Education & Capacity Building

📋

Training & Certification Programs

  • Short-term and long-term programs on water and air quality monitoring
  • Hands-on training modules aligned with industry practices
  • Workshops on environmental sampling and analysis techniques
📚

Academic Courses & Add-ons

  • Environmental Chemistry and Pollution Control modules
  • Water Treatment Technology elective courses
  • Integration of real case studies from regional monitoring data
🏫

Student Exposure & Internships

  • Practical laboratory training on analytical instruments
  • Field visits to water treatment plants and monitoring stations
  • Dissertation and project supervision support

Research & Analytical Services

🔬

Sponsored Research Projects

  • Water and air quality studies in urban and rural environments
  • Development of cost-effective pollutant removal technologies
  • Assessment of heavy metals and organic pollutants in aquatic systems
🧪

Testing & Analysis Laboratory

  • Physical, chemical, and biological water quality parameters
  • Air pollutant and particulate matter measurement
  • COD, BOD, heavy metals, and microbiological testing
📄

Consultancy Services

  • Environmental impact assessment support
  • Wastewater treatment process evaluation
  • Pollution source identification and monitoring design

Industry Collaboration & Outreach

🏭

Industry–Academia Projects

  • Joint research with environmental agencies and NGOs
  • Live consultancy and industry problem-solving platforms
  • Technology transfer for environmental monitoring solutions
🌍

Community & Policy Outreach

  • Public awareness campaigns on water and air quality
  • Scientific data support for environmental policy formulation
  • National seminars and expert lecture programs
📰

Publications & Reports

  • Research papers in peer-reviewed journals
  • Technical reports and monitoring data documentation
  • Best-practice guidelines for environmental management
